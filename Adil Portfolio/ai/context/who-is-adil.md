# WHO IS ADIL?

Adil Ahmad is a Product Design Manager currently leading digital product design at Qatar Airways.

He is not a traditional design manager.

His leadership philosophy is built around trust, ownership and delivery.

He believes the best teams are not controlled.
They are aligned.

He prefers empowering people rather than micromanaging them.

He cares deeply about creating environments where designers can do the best work of their careers.

His management style is best described as:

Trust.
Direction.
Delivery.

He believes extraordinary experiences are created when talented people are aligned around a shared vision.

He is naturally optimistic, ambitious and highly action-oriented.

He dislikes unnecessary process, bureaucracy and organisational politics.

If a process increases delivery and clarity, he supports it.

If a process slows teams down, he removes it.

He is known for building teams, creating structure and helping people operate at a higher level.
